FAQ

How long does it take to resolve a delivery issue?

Most delivery issues are resolved within 24-48 hours. Complex cases may take up to 5 business days.

What evidence should I provide for order disputes?

Please provide clear photos of the issue, packaging if relevant, and any other documentation that supports your claim.

How are refunds processed?

Refunds are typically processed within 3-5 business days and will be credited to the original payment method.

What's the best way to prevent order issues?

Double-check orders before handing them to delivery partners, ensure proper packaging, and maintain clear communication with customers.
